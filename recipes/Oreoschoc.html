Ingredients

  • 1 (14 ounce) package chocolate sandwich cookies (such as Oreo速)
  • 12 ounces red candy melts
  • multicolored sprinkles (jimmies)

Direction

    step 1

  1. Place candy melts in a microwave-safe bowl. Microwave for 1 minute. Stir. Microwave for 30 seconds more. Stir. Repeat process until candy melts are completely liquid...

    2. step 2

  2. Dip sandwich cookies into the melted candy melts - feel free to coat only half of the cookie, or coat all sides. Place red cookies on a plate lined with wax paper.

    3. step 3

  3. Decorate with assorted sprinkles. Allow to harden before removing to serve.
